Beacon Support

Beacons !?

  • Bluetooth LE devices
  • Broadcast a signal at a defined interval with a defined data packet
  • Signal has a range of up to 100m for a normal beacon, some go as far as 500m
  • They don't rely on a client connection, just a regular, public broadcast
  • Low power - some run off USB, some run of batteries with lieftimes in months to years
  • In contrast to GPS, beacons provide more accurate information for in-door localization
